Crossroads Dining Commons, 2415 Bowditch Street. Berkeley Dining's flagship commons with a modern atmosphere, recognized for international cuisine and strong flavors. It serves around 4,400 students daily. Features 10 stations offering options including pho, pizza, deli, and a salad bar. Emphasizes close attention to food labels, allergen-free platforms, and dietary needs. A recommended item is the Lemon Grass (Pho). For hours, the page directs visitors to check the hours listing.
